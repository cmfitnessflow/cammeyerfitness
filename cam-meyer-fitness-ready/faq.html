FAQ

Clear answers before you get started.

Personal training should feel straightforward, not intimidating.

Do I need to already be in shape?

No. Cam works with beginners, active adults, and older clients. Training starts where you are.

Where does training happen?

Most sessions happen in your home or in your downtown St. Petersburg condo fitness facility.

What areas do you serve?

Cam Meyer Fitness serves Downtown St. Pete, Old Northeast, Snell Isle, Historic Kenwood, Crescent Lake, and Tropicana Field/Edge District.

Can you train clients online?

Cam trains mostly in person, but online sessions may be available for traveling clients or clients who are not local.

What happens first?

You start with a conversation about medical history, injuries, goals, schedule, and fit. Then Cam leads a shortened workout to see how you move.

What if I have injuries or mobility concerns?

That is part of the conversation. Cam adjusts training around your body, limitations, recovery, and comfort level.

Still have a question?

Call Cam directly and get a clear next step.
