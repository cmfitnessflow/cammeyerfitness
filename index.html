About Cam

Capable coaching that meets you where you are.

Whether you are brand new to fitness, returning after time away, or trying to stay active for the long run, Cam keeps the process personal, practical, and sustainable.

Every new client starts with a conversation about goals, medical history, injuries, schedule, and lifestyle. From there, Cam leads a shortened movement session to understand how you move and whether the fit is right on both sides.
