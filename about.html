About Cam

A coach who understands the starting line.

My approach to training comes from personal experience, professional education, and seven years working exclusively in fitness and gym management.

My Story

Fitness did not always feel easy to me.

I became a trainer because I know what it is like to struggle with healthy habits around food, exercise, and feeling comfortable in your own skin.

Over time, I learned that lasting health is not built through extreme plans. It is built through small habits repeated consistently. That realization changed my life, led me to leave a career in technology, and pushed me to pursue personal training through NASM.

Today, I help clients build strength, mobility, and confidence in a way that feels approachable and sustainable.
