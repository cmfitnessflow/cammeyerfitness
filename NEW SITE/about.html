About Cam

Personal training that feels professional, calm, and built around you.

Cam Meyer Fitness is for people who want to get stronger, move better, and build confidence without being thrown into a one-size-fits-all gym routine.

A better entry point into fitness.

Cam works with beginners who feel intimidated by the gym, active adults who want to keep moving for longer, and older clients who want better balance, mobility, and strength.

His coaching style is capable, caring, and direct. You will know what you are doing, why it matters, and how each session supports your long-term health.

Training can happen in your home, in your downtown St. Petersburg condo fitness facility, or online when travel or distance makes that the best option.
